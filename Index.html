Certify Your Skills

Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) is a process where individuals can gain recognition for the skills and knowledge they have acquired through formal, non-formal, and informal learning experiences.

– Step 1 –

Free Skills Assessment

Engage in a conversation with us regarding your aspirations, and let us lead you towards the qualification that will pave the way for your desired destination.

– Step 2 –

Portfolio of Experience

Your committed consultant will assist you in assembling a portfolio of evidence, which may include international qualifications, transcripts, certificates, photos, videos, work samples, letters of reference, your existing resume, and more.

– Step 3 –

Evidence Review

After one of our affiliated Registered Training Organizations (RTOs) evaluates your portfolio, the designated assessor may contact you for any supplementary evidence if needed. If there are any areas of weakness, the assessor help you overcome those weakness.

– Step 4 –

You’re qualified

We are partnered with Registered Training Organizations (RTOs) to give you the best opportunities to take your experiences and prior learning and turn them into a nationally-recognized qualification through the Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL).

The Benefits of RPL

Every individual's skills and career path are distinct. Therefore, the requirements for upskilling and bridging knowledge gaps are also unique. We approach each case with dedicated, personalized attention to ensure you can make informed decisions tailored to your career advancement.

Save Time

Skip years of traditional study. With Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL), you can finish in weeks, bypassing the need to study one or all units of your qualification.

Save Money

RPL offers a budget-friendly route to attaining your qualification, enabling you to significantly reduce course expenses.

Save Effort

By acknowledging your existing knowledge, you can bypass redundant learning and classroom sessions, all from the convenience of your home through online recognition.
