Certify Your Skills

Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) is a process where individuals can gain recognition for the skills and knowledge they have acquired through formal, non-formal, and informal learning experiences.

Our Process

Free Skills Assessment

Engage in a conversation with RPL Australia regarding your aspirations, and let us lead you towards the qualification that will pave the way for your desired destination.

Portfolio Of Experience

Your committed consultant will assist you in assembling a portfolio of evidence, which may include international qualifications, transcripts, certificates, photos, videos, work samples, letters of reference, your existing resume, and more.

Evidence Review

After one of our affiliated Registered Training Organizations (RTOs) evaluates your portfolio, the designated assessor may contact you for any supplementary evidence if needed. If there are any areas of weakness, the assessor help you overcome those weakness.

You’re Qualified

RPL Australia collaborates with Registered Training Organizations whose certifications hold national recognition in Australia, equivalent to those obtained through full-time study. These qualifications are valuable for licensing or immigration purposes as well.

The Benefits of RPL

Your skills and career are unique. So, your needs of upskilling and filling the gaps in your knowledge will be unique as well. At Skills Certified Australia, we look at your case in a dedicated and personalised way so that you can take the right next steps for your career.

Save Time

Forget years of study! RPL can be completed within weeks, exempting you from studying one or all the units of your qualification.

Save Money

RPL is a cost-effective way of achieving your qualification, allowing you to save a substantial amount off course fees.

Save Effort

Avoid repeat learning and classroom study by recognizing what you already know, online and from the comfort of your home.
